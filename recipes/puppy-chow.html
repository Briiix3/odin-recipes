Puppy Chow

Description

This is a nice and easy treat/dessert to make.

Ingredients

  • Chex mix
  • Semi sweet chocolate chips
  • Peanut butter
  • Powdered sugar

Steps

  1. First you need to melt equal parts peanut butter and chocolate chips in a bowl.
  2. Once melted stir in the chex mix until they are all fully covered.
  3. Wait til cool and then shake in a bag with powdered sugar.
  4. And they are now ready to enjoy!
