Chilli

Description

This is the worlds best chilli! It is also a great vegetarian option.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of kidney beans
  • olive oil
  • oregano
  • basil
  • salt

Steps

  1. First you need to add a little bit of olive oil to a pot on medium heat.
  2. Add tomato paste, tomato sauce and seasonings.
  3. Add both cans of beans.
  4. Stir and cover pot.
  5. Cook for 30 minutes on low heat.
  6. And serve in a bowl.
