Baked Pasta

Description

This is the worlds best baked pasta and my favorite pasta dish! It is also a great vegetarian option.

Ingredients

  • 1 box of pasta (your choice)
  • 1 jar of RAO pasta sauce
  • sharp cheddar shredded cheese
  • oregano
  • basil
  • salt

Steps

  1. First you need to boil your choice of pasta until it is done.
  2. Take your cooked pasta and drain it.
  3. Mix your pasta and sauce in a oven safe cooking dish.
  4. Add as much basil, salt and oregano as you like to the dish.
  5. Top the dish with shredded cheese.
  6. Bake in oven at 350 for 20-30 minutes.
